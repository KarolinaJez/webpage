About me

From a chemist to an aspiring teacher to an NLP specialist. Here is my story.

Lying before me were many paths as I tried to find my way in life. I let my instincts guide me and I began my journey by attending the Gdańsk University of Technology. Though it turned out that being a scientist did not suit me as I hoped it would, the brief experience taught me many valuable life lessons, preparing me for challenges to come. It is there I have nurtured my leadership skills, learned how to solve and overcome difficult issues and became more systematic in my work. Still, I moved on. The second stop was the University of Gdańsk. There I got my BA degree in English Philology whith an initial goal of becoming a teacher, a goal which gradually became irrelevant as I decided that being a teacher would not be my first career choice after all. The reason for that is that during my studies I have discovered other areas of interest, which eventually led to me choosing an NLP specialisation for my MA studies.

It is this path that I follow now and it is by far the most promising. I am a quick learner and the two previous paths have prepared me for what lies ahead on this journey that has just begun.
